BLACKPINK IN YOUR AREA

BLACKPINK (블랙핑크) consists of 4 members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. The band debuted on August 8th, 2016 with their first single album “Square One” under YG Entertainment. On October 23, 2018, BLACKPINK officially signed with the U.S. label Interscope Records.
